🌍 What is FedWatch?

FedWatch is a free, offline-ready, community-powered flood response and reporting system. It helps rural citizens and local officials track flooding impacts, alert responders, and restore environmental data lost during emergencies.

🚀 Key Features

  • 🌐 Works offline and syncs automatically
  • 📱 Installable on Android & iOS (no app store required)
  • 📍 Report with GPS, timestamp, and severity
  • 📊 Local dashboards for responders
  • ⚠️ Automatic alerts when thresholds are crossed

🛠️ How to Use

  1. Click “Submit Flood Report” to report incidents
  2. Click “View Dashboard” to see community alerts
  3. Tap “Install” (in browser) to use it offline like an app

💡 Why It Matters

During floods, internet access, infrastructure, and national systems often collapse. FedWatch works offline with a phone browser — making it ideal for emergency field response in remote or disaster-affected areas.
