The FedWatch (FloodTrackerApp) is a public prototype developed to support flood awareness, health alerts, and education disruptions in disaster-prone or low-connectivity regions. This application is hosted for public demonstration and testing purposes as part of the HeroX Data-to-Dust Challenge.

1. Data Collection

This app does not collect any personally identifiable information (PII) such as names, phone numbers, GPS location, or email addresses.

Reports submitted (e.g., flood, health, education) may include date, zone/area, and type of observation. All submissions are anonymous and stored locally in your browser or offline app cache.

2. Cookies and Analytics

This site does not use cookies, trackers, or third-party analytics tools. No browsing behavior is monitored, shared, or sold.

3. Offline Storage

As a Progressive Web App (PWA), FedWatch stores certain assets (like forms or templates) locally to enable offline use. These assets do not contain personal data.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses open-source libraries (e.g., Leaflet.js for maps). Map tiles may come from public providers like OpenStreetMap, which may log basic technical data (e.g., IP address, browser type) for security and performance.

5. Intellectual Property

All submissions remain the intellectual property of the submitter. By submitting data, users grant the developers permission to use aggregated, anonymized information for research, development, or public health purposes.

6. Updates and Revisions

This Privacy Policy may be updated periodically to reflect changes in the app or to align with HeroX challenge requirements. The latest version will always appear on this page.
